Meghan Domeck

Meghan is a software developer from Ohio! She enjoys fixing things and solving problems. She is proactive about improving systems and willing to collaborate and work as part of a team. Software development became and interest after faced with a career change opportunity. She enjoys the challenge and impact that is made working in the coding field. She is also found spending time with family and friends. Some of her interests are knitting, jigsaw puzzles and playing cello.

Matt Herriges

Matt Herriges (rhymes with asparagus) was born in Tacoma, WA in 1994 and currently still lives in the area in Kent. Chandler Puckett

Touring with artists such as Tool, Demi Lovato, 21 Savage, Nick Jonas and many more.
